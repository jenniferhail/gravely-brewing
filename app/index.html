Craft beer. Craft music.

Beer and music go together like, well, music and beer. You can have one without the other, but why? At Gravely, we’ve combined those two passions to make a music brewery. A place where delicious, thirst-quenching beer is served alongside a healthy shot of soul‑sustaining music.

Who we are

beer

Our beers

At the top of the beer chain.

Our beer styles vary as much as our musical selections. From “holy crap that’s hoppy!” to “finally a good lager in this town!” you’ll find something that hits your sweet spot. We have 14 taps and frequently feature our “Greatest Hits” beers, while the rest are variations or seasonal varieties.
